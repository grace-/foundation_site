Helping the OpenCV Library

Whether you are an individual or a corporation, you can help the OpenCV library in many ways as detailed on the Contribute page of the OpenCV library website.

OpenCV Supporting Member

By becoming a supporting member of the OpenCV foundation, you help financially. Through your financial contribution, the OpenCV foundation supports:

  • events meant to improve computer vision: workshops, brainstorm session, developer sessions
  • prizes to promote research, code or computer vision activities
  • infrastructure (e.g. web servers, build farms, administrative fees ...)
  • development of the OpenCV library

Any contribution can be made through the "DONATE" link on top of the OpenCV website or by purchasing goodies on our Store.

Special Supporting Member

You can also become a Member of OpenCV by paying a yearly membership fee to the OpenCV foundation:

  • $100 for individuals (that includes a personalized OpenCV gift)
  • $1000 for corporations

You are then entitled to display the Member of OpenCV logo on your website or in printed materials. This membership also gives you the right to be heard at the board meetings but with no voting right. You could use that right to suggest any feature/direction for the foundation (e.g. if you want to sponsor an event or a feature in the OpenCV library)

Corporations wishing to show exceptional participation to the OpenCV foundation can become a Patron of OpenCV by extending their membership fees:

  • $5000 for small companies (less than 50 employees)
  • $10000 for corporations

Patrons of OpenCV get the same features as the Members of OpenCV but also get a voting seat on the OpenCV foundation board of directors.

To register as a Member of OpenCV or Patron of OpenCV, please fill the form and send it to us.
